## Introduction

Cashooya Playground ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Financial Transaction Data
- **Receipt images** you capture or select from your photo library
- **Transaction details** extracted from receipts (merchant name, amount, date, items)
- **Categories and notes** you assign to transactions
- **Spending patterns and analytics** generated from your transaction history

### 2. Device Information
- **Camera access** for receipt scanning functionality
- **Photo library access** for selecting existing receipt images
- **Device storage** for local data persistence

### 3. Technical Data
- **App usage analytics** (crashes, performance metrics)
- **API interaction logs** for receipt processing

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- **Receipt Analysis**: Process receipt images to extract transaction details
- **Expense Tracking**: Store and categorize your financial transactions
- **Analytics**: Provide spending insights and financial summaries
- **App Functionality**: Enable core features like receipt scanning and data management

### Data Processing
- **Local Storage**: Transaction data is stored locally on your device using secure iOS data storage
- **AI Processing**: Receipt images are temporarily sent to third-party AI services for text extraction and analysis
- **No Account Required**: We do not require user accounts or collect personal identification

## Third-Party Services

### AI Receipt Processing
- We use **OpenRouter/OpenAI services** to analyze receipt images
- Receipt images are temporarily transmitted to these services for processing
- Images are not permanently stored by third-party services
- Extracted text data is returned to your device and the image is discarded

### Analytics (Optional)
- **Firebase Analytics** (if enabled) for app performance monitoring
- Anonymous usage statistics to improve app functionality
- No personally identifiable information is transmitted

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All transaction data is stored **locally on your device**
- We use iOS secure storage mechanisms (SwiftData, Keychain)
- API keys are stored securely in iOS Keychain
- Data is encrypted using iOS standard encryption

### Data Retention
- Transaction data remains on your device until you delete it
- Receipt images are processed temporarily and not permanently stored
- You have full control over your data deletion

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control
- **View Data**: Access all your stored transaction data within the app
- **Edit Data**: Modify or update transaction details at any time
- **Delete Data**: Remove individual transactions or clear all data
- **Export Data**: Generate reports of your financial data

### Permissions
- **Camera**: Required only for receipt scanning - you can deny and still use manual entry
- **Photo Library**: Required only for selecting existing receipt images
- **Notifications**: Optional for transaction reminders

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

## International Users

If you are using our app outside the United States, please note that your data may be processed in countries where our third-party services operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.
